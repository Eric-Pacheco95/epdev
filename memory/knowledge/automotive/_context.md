## Cross-Cutting Themes

- Trade policy volatility (tariffs) is a primary risk variable for Chinese EV pricing and availability in Canada -- the 6.1% tariff rate is a policy outcome, not a structural floor
- Incentive eligibility (EVAP/iZEV) is as important as sticker price in Canadian EV decisions; assembly origin determines rebate access
- Range figures (WLTP) are European-standard and will not reflect Canadian winter performance; real-world range is typically 15-30% lower in cold climates
