# Cooking Domain Knowledge

Last updated: 2026-04-18

## Domain Overview

Culinary techniques, pairings, and Eric's cooking preferences. Scoped to **method fluency** over recipe collection — the goal is pantry-aware improvisation, not a cookbook archive.

Loaded when topics include: cooking, recipe, pairing, spice, technique, protein, starch, sauce, ingredient, flavor, kitchen, chef, cuisine, pantry.

## Sub-Domains

- `techniques.md` — core cooking methods (pilaf, sear-rest, braise, pan sauce, emulsion, etc.) — the WHY behind each
- `pairings.md` — protein ↔ starch ↔ spice/cuisine compatibility matrix
- `eric-preferences.md` — Eric's palate, equipment, skill level, pantry snapshot

## Cross-Cutting Themes

- **Technique > recipe**: understanding a method (e.g., spice blooming in fat) unlocks N recipes; memorizing one recipe unlocks 1
- **Aromatics-in-fat is universal**: garlic/onion/ginger/whole-spices sautéed in butter or oil is the first move of most cuisines
- **Acid finishes**: the home-cook-to-restaurant gap is usually a squeeze of lemon/lime or splash of vinegar at the end
- **Rest matters**: proteins keep cooking after pulled; rice keeps steaming after heat off — undercook slightly + rest covered
