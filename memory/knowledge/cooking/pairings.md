# Pairings — Protein ↔ Starch ↔ Cuisine Matrix

> Living reference. Grows as Eric cooks more dishes. Goal: given what's in the fridge/pantry, quickly see 3 directions.

## Chicken — Rice Pairings by Cuisine

| Cuisine | Spice/Flavor Combo | Finishing Touch | Notes |
|---------|-------------------|-----------------|-------|
| **Simplest** | Butter + garlic + parsley | Lemon, salt | Zero-risk default. Pairs with any chicken prep. |
| **Mexican-leaning** | Butter + garlic + chile-lime blend + cinnamon | Lime, cilantro | Eric's 2026-04-18 improvised combo. Warm + citrus + heat. |
| **Mediterranean** | Olive oil + garlic + oregano + lemon zest | Parsley, feta crumble | Pairs with grilled or roasted chicken. |
| **Middle Eastern (yellow rice)** | Butter + turmeric + cumin + cinnamon stick + bay | Toasted almonds, parsley | Classic with roasted or braised chicken. |
| **South Asian pulao** | Ghee + cumin seed + cardamom + clove + cinnamon + bay | Cilantro, fried onion | Pairs with tandoori-style or curry chicken. |
| **Latin** | Olive oil + cumin + smoked paprika + garlic | Lime, cilantro | Pairs with grilled chicken, fajita-style. |
| **Cajun** | Butter + paprika + thyme + garlic powder + cayenne | Green onion | Pairs with blackened or pan-seared chicken. |
| **Asian (jasmine)** | Coconut oil + ginger + lemongrass + kaffir lime | Fresh herbs (basil, cilantro) | Pairs with soy/ginger chicken. |

## Universal Pairing Rules

- **Warm spices (cinnamon, clove, cardamom, nutmeg)** pair with chicken in almost every cuisine — don't fear them in savory dishes
- **Acid at the end** (lemon, lime, vinegar) brightens heavy/fatty dishes — almost always an upgrade
- **Fresh herbs at the end** (parsley, cilantro, basil) add a "top note" that dried herbs can't
- **Chicken fat is mild** — it takes on the profile of what you cook it in; season aggressively

## Starch Swap Logic

| Want to swap rice for... | Works with same spice combo? |
|--------------------------|------------------------------|
| Couscous / orzo | Yes — use same bloom-then-liquid method |
| Quinoa | Yes — toasts well, holds flavor |
| Roasted potatoes | Mostly — cumin/paprika/garlic great; lemon-herb great; avoid cardamom-heavy blends |
| Flatbread / naan | Cuisine pairing > exact spice match |
| Pasta | Mediterranean & Cajun transfer; South Asian / Middle Eastern don't |
