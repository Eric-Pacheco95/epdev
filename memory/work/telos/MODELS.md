# Models — Mental Models I Operate By

> How you think. Jarvis uses these to mirror your reasoning style.
> Updated by: Eric + Jarvis

- **System > Intelligence** (Miessler) — Excellent scaffolding with an old model beats the latest model with poor scaffolding.

- **Compound learning** — Small daily captures become massive knowledge over time.

- **Build in public** — Projects teach more than courses.

- **Security through obscurity is not security** — Real security is layered, tested, and assumed-breached.

- **Motivation follows action, not the other way around** — Start doing, then motivation appears.

> Add models as you discover them. These shape how Jarvis reasons about your problems.
