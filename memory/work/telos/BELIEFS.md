# Beliefs — Core Values and Worldview

> Non-negotiable principles that guide decisions. Jarvis uses these as constraints.
> Updated by: Eric (rare)

- **Security is non-negotiable** — Fear of government overreach and surveillance is rational. Privacy and operational security matter deeply.

- **Continuous learning is life** — Always be acquiring new skills, trying new things, building new projects.

- **AI should integrate safely with human consciousness** — Not replace human thinking, but augment it. The human stays in the loop.

- **Building is joy** — The creative act of making things (code, music, systems, businesses) is where flow state lives.

- **Systems beat motivation** — Discipline and scaffolding matter more than willpower, especially for health/gym.

- **Skill expression is joy** — I am an ex-professional call of duty player and currently still play competitive fortnite. I also play lead guitar in a band and consider myself very skillful at certain tasks. Being able to master your crafts, put in the work to getting better and executing as expected brings true pleasure
